Doctors

DR. DEAN SALO

Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) – Implantology; Cosmetic & Reconstructive Dentistry; TMD Disorders;

Dr. Dean Salo passionately believes cosmetic dentistry is an art form. Dr. Salo has been taught by well-known dentistry leaders including Dr. Michael Pikos and Dr. John Kois. He has completed various training at The Dawson Academy and Las Vegas Institute for Advanced Dental Studies. Dr. Dean Salo is a perfectionist with each of his patients. He focuses on providing a natural look and feel design on his restorations. Dr. Salo makes dentistry his art by utilizes ancient techniques like divine proportions with new technologies including the T-scan, for the perfect bite. He includes most updated dentistry technologies like lasers, CT scan, and digital radiography. Dr. Salo uses these to fit his patients’ needs and provide the highest level of patient comfort.

Dr. Dean Salo became interested in cosmetic restorations while working in a dental lab while attending UCLA. In 1989, he graduated as the youngest student in his class with a Bachelors of Science degree in Biochemistry. Dr. Dean graduated at 24 with a DDS from University of Pacific’s Arthur A. Dugoni School of Dentistry. He provides excellent service to many famous faces in Los Angeles and patients seeking top-quality personalized patient care. Dr. Salo has an integrative practice located in South Bay and Miracle Mile and leads some of the best dental professionals in Los Angeles. In addition to his extensive experience, Dr. Dean Salo supervises, practices dentistry, and has earned privileges at the well-known General Residency Program at Cedars Sinai Hospital.

DR. RENATE GANGEL

Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) – Cosmetic & General Dentistry

Dr. Renate Gangel graduated in 1986 from the Herman Ostrow School of Dentistry of USC. Her career in dentistry derives from her excellence in patient care. Dr. Gangel sets high standards for her care while still providing a personal feel to her patients. She has a natural talent for calming patients’ nerves. Dr. Renate has widespread knowledge of dentistry including but not limited to cosmetic restorations, prosthetics creations, and routine care. She enjoys caring for patients with special needs and elderly patients because they require specific care.

Dr. Renate has a passion for learning. She attends multiple courses, lectures, and seminars and is eager to further her knowledge of dentistry. Dr. Gangel is attentive to the newest dental technological advancements. She uses digital x-ray diagnoses which helps her patients avoid high levels of radiation and helps her give a better diagnosis. Additionally, Dr. Gangel has trained and is certified in the use of soft tissue lasers.

Dr. Gangel was born in Buenos Aires in Argentina. At 3 years old, she moved to Long Beach, California. Long Beach became Dr. Renate’s home where she graduated from California State University, Long Beach with a degree in Biology. Currently, Dr. Renate Gangel lives in Lakewood with Annie, her daughter, and her two dogs. When she’s not practicing dentistry, she’s enjoying decorating, gardening, and cooking.

DR. SAMANTHA BESSER

Doctor of Medicine in Dentistry (DMD) – Board Eligible Endodontist

Many of Dr. Samantha Besser’s patients are charmed by her friendly personality and warming smile. Many are shocked to learn that Dr. Besser is a U.S. Air Force honorary officer and spent 5 years providing dental care to our troops both abroad and at home.

Dr. Samantha studied at the University of California, Santa Barbara then transferred to Tufts University School of Dental Medicine in Boston. There, she received her DMD degree. Once she earned her DMD degree, Dr. Samantha Besser served as a Dental Officer in the U.S. Air Force. After serving in the military,

Dr. Besser practiced as a general dentist with a passion for Endodontics. After only a year of being a dental practitioner, she returned to school. She entered a competitive, male-dominated, and highly renowned USC School of Dentistry’s Endodontic program. There, she earned her Endodontic certification, after 2 years of meticulous training, and became a Board Eligible Endodontist.

Dr. Samantha Besser uses only the most progressive equipment and tools when treating her patients. She may have a gentle manner, but her skill and confidence levels are incomparable. She has a dedication to her patients and makes sure their care is at the highest possible level. Dr. Besser makes sure that her patients’ experience is as comfortable and non-traumatic as possible by providing multiple forms of anesthesia. Dr. Besser is experienced in treating pediatric patients and patients with disabilities.

Dr. Samantha Besser, native to California, lives in Santa Monica with Sean, her husband.

DR. AFSHIN SALAMATI

Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) – Masters of Science (MS)

Dr. Afshin Salamati earned his DDS in 1996 from UCLA. After, he continued his education at UCLA and specialized in dental implant surgery and periodontics where he earned his MS degree in 1999.

Dr. Salamati started a dental practice in 2003 in Beverly Hills, and since then has maintained his dental implants and periodontics private practice. After graduating, Dr. Afshin, also, teaches at UCLA. While attending UCLA, Dr. Afshin Salamati participated in multiple clinical and laboratory trials.

Dr. Afshin has a maximum rank of success in periodontics by being a member of the American Board of Periodontology. This achievement has been attained by only the best periodontists.

DR. ALEX BRANDON

DDS, MD

Dr. Alex Brandon is the son of an orthodontist. While in high school, he started his dental training in Seattle by assisting his father. In order to pay for his University of Washington undergraduate education, he worked as a charter boat skipper. At the University of San Francisco is where Dr. Alex began his dental academic career, and then later earned his DDS degree. After earning his degree, he graduated from Columbia University’s orthodontic program in 1996.

For over 10 years Dr. Alex Brandon has been creating healthy, functional, beautiful smiles. He always builds unique bonds with his patients, both adults, and children. Dr. Brandon’s personality is outgoing and friendly which helps smooth the orthodontic process for his patients. He builds his procedures on establishing functional bite, placing TMJ in a natural position, and improving dental and facial esthetics. For Dr. Brandon to provide the best treatment possible, he thoroughly evaluates his patients’ dental health, stresses the importance of practicing proper oral hygiene through the prevention of cavities and the maintenance of healthy gums.

Dr. Alex Brandon keeps his skills pristine and provides the best possible results and comfort to his patients. He regularly attends seminars and classes that will keep him up-to-date on the latest developments and advancements in the dental industry. He is an active member of the American Orthodontist Association and belongs to other professional organizations.

Dr. Brandon and his wife are a match made in dental heaven. They met in dental school and live in Southern California with their two black labs, Lily and Grace. They spend most of their leisure time in Dana Point on their charter boat, DOGHOUSE. Dr. Alex Brandon’s greatest enjoyments are scuba diving and fishing, but he also loves to build remote-controlled helicopters, fabricate parts, and weld.

DR. ANDREW H. CHEN

Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) – Fellow of the American Dental Society of Anesthesia (FADSA)

Dr. Andrew Chen graduated with his DDS Degree in 1992 from The University of Southern California. He completed his 2-year residency program of anesthesiology in 1994 from the University of Pittsburgh. Dr. Andrew Chen has published research in multiple trade publications as well as many academic appointments. He is a member of multiple professional societies and organizations including the American Dental Board of Anesthesiology, the National Dental Board of Anesthesiology. Dr. Chen has also earned a FADSA and is an Advanced Cardiac Life Support Instructor for the American Heart Association.

Dr. Chen is dedicated to anesthesiology services and is a firm believer of equal access to dental care. He believes no patient should be limited because of their fears or health conditions and treats patients who have dental phobias, small children, and patients with physical limitations. Dr. Andrew studies each of his patient’s history to plan the best possible treatment. He works with his patients’ physicians to make any arrangements necessary to ensure his patients’ success and safety of the procedure. Dr. Andrew Chen has outstanding chair side manners, gentleness, and skills that make him a top anesthesiology specialist and is the perfect addition to our team.

DR. OSCAR ALONZO

DDS, MD – Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeon

Oscar Alonzo, DDS, MD

Beverly Hills & Los Angeles Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeon

Oscar Alonzo, DDS, MD is a native of the San Francisco Bay Area, where he completed his undergraduate training at San Francisco State University, obtaining a bachelor’s degree in Psychology.

Dr. Alonzo, obtained his DDS degree from the prestigious University of California, San Francisco, which he followed with an esteemed postgraduate residency training program in Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery at the Los Angeles County-Martin Luther King Jr. / Drew Medical Center.

Upon graduating from his residency, Dr. Alonzo went on to medical school training at the University of California, Los Angeles/Charles R. Drew University of Medicine and Science program. He completed a one-year general surgery internship at the University of California, Davis/East Bay in Oakland, California. He also has certification in Basic and Advanced Cardiac Life Support as well as Pediatric Advanced Life Support.

Dr. Alonzo has over twenty-five years of private practice experience as an Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeon in Southern California and has over twenty years of experience as a General Anesthesia Evaluator for the State of California.

Dr. Alonzo values the importance of maintaining active participation in organized dentistry and the academic community in California and abroad. He is a fellow of the American Dental Society of Anesthesiology, a diplomat of the American Dental Board of Anesthesiology, and an active member in good standing with the California Association of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeons, and the American Association of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeons.

Dr. Alonzo’s clinical interests include wisdom teeth extraction, dental implants, oral pathology and bone grafting procedures. He has extensive expertise in anesthesia, which allows him to treat patients safely and comfortably in a controlled environment.

He greatly enjoys public speaking and educating his patients. Dr. Alonzo will always take the time to explain any aspect of a disease, condition or treatment to his patients and their family. Dr. Alonzo feels that it is critical that each patient has a complete understanding of treatment they are about to have, and utilizes visual aids, videos, and simple terminology to ensure that all questions are answered prior to all procedures. Soothing his patients and their family, while exhibiting genuine empathy are critical cornerstones in Dr. Alonzo’s approach to building a solid doctor-patient relationship.

He greatly appreciates the trust you and your family place in him and he looks forward to meeting all of your Oral and Maxillofacial surgical needs.
